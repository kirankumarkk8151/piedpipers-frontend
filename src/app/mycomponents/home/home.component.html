Team PiedPipers is a departmental coding club of Master of Computer Applications, Siddaganga Institute of Technology,Tumkur. We organize various technical activities at regular intervals. This club is a platform where Tech enthusiasts with different interests such as, coding, App development, Web Designing etc can interact with like-minded peers and explore the world of computer science. The club is responsible for conducting various technical and non technical events for the students of Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumakuru.
